# The-Bullfrog-Sandwich
The best sandwich recipe in the world! Vegan, healthy and delicious. 

## Ingredients
- Ciabatta bread  
- Ithaca Lemon Beet Hummus (available at most grocery stores)  
- Fresh basil  
- Avocado  
- Sweet piquante peppers (in the grocery store pickled section)  
- Salt  
- Pepper  
- Red chili flakes  
- 1/2 lemon  
- Cucumber  

## Instructions

### Step 1
Mash the avocado with:
- Lemon juice  
- Salt  
- Pepper  
- Chili flakes  

### Step 2
Tear up the basil leaves.

### Step 3
- Chop up the piquante peppers  
- Slice the cucumber thin  

### Step 4
- Spread hummus on the bottom slice of bread  
- Add basil and piquante peppers  
- Spread the avocado mixture on the top slice  
- Place cucumber slices into the avocado layer  
- Put the two halves together  

## Enjoy!
